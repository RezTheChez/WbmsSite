Apple Drop

By Charles

In this engaging arcade-style game written by Charles Baker, you control a bowl using the arrow keys. Your job is to catch the falling apples while dodging the dangerous spikes also dropping down. It's all about moving fast and smart to grab apples and avoid getting spiked! As you play, things get trickier, so you've got to stay sharp!


Controls: Arrow Keys
